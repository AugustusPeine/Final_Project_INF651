About

This interactive calendar is a dynamic web application that allows users to manage their tasks and due dates effectively. Users can add, edit, describe, and delete tasks on any day on the calendar. They can also view upcoming and past tasks in the dashboard. Tasks are color-coded to indicate past due and upcoming dates. The dashboard provides an overview of all tasks and supports real-time search by task title, description, or date. The calendar updates instantly when users change months, years, or task details. Navigation between pages—including Calendar, Dashboard, and About—is smooth and dynamic.
